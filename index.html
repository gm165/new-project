Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect the information you provide when submitting a visa application, including your name, passport number, contact details, and supporting documents. When you use the tracking system, we process the passport and reference numbers you enter solely to retrieve your application status.

2. How we use your information

Your information is used to process your application, communicate updates, confirm payment status, and generate your approval letter. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Data security

All data submitted through this website is transmitted using encrypted connections. Access to client records is restricted to authorized staff through the administrative dashboard.

4. Data retention

We retain application records for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose they were collected for, and as required by applicable regulations.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting our support team. Where applicable local regulations or GDPR apply, additional rights may be available to you.

6. Contact us

For any privacy-related questions, please reach out through our support channels listed on the FAQs page.
